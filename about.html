About HexR

HexR (Pronounced Hex-Er) was founded on April 13, 2014 with a goal. To write code for websites. That's what we're really about. But it's also nice to know what makes us different. Here, you can find the answer to the question Why should I choose to have my website written by HexR?

Well, here are a few reasons why:

  • HexR is 100% non-profit
    • In fact, HexR is a zero-dollar organization. That means we don't spend or earn ANY money. Some of the donations we recieve go straight to our sponsors as a token of gratitude, and the rest is given to STEM clubs and organizations in local high schools
    • This means that our goal is getting you a website that works in a timely manner. Our only focus is you.
  • HexR is simple
    • The code we write is no nonsense. We don't waste code. The backbone of the site is minimal. We don't need fancy stuff to produce modern sites
    • Check out our code and you will notice that we've done a lot to make sure that this website is small and loads fast.
  • We're adaptive
    • If you already have a website, we can use it to update it to modern standards
    • We know a variety of framweorks, and if we don't know yours, we're willing to learn it.
    • Our goal is to produce your website your way. No shortcuts.
  • It's FREE
    • The only time you pay us is if you feel that you want to donate to the cause. You can donate here.
    • The only price you pay is what you need - hosting, domains, emails, etc. Our plans show you what you might need to pay.
  • We don't discriminate against anything
    • First and foremost, almost all business is done online. That means we don't discriminate by race or gender or anything in between
    • We also accept new coders, old coders, bad coders and no coders. Our goal is to provide you with a website. If you want to make one yourself, we plan on starting a video series explaining different languages, and you are free to contact us with questions.

If you don't understand something or want to know more, please feel free to contact us and we will work to explain it to you. We truely care about your business because the world deserves websites that are modern, simple and change the world.


HexR. The only organization fully dedicated to producing the best websites for everyone. We're ready to take any challenge you throw at us, from a small single page site to a large ten thousand page site. From the fearing individual to the tech savvy engineer. Whoever you are and whatever you need, we're ready to help you. Let's get started!
